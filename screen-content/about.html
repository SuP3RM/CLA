About Chicago's Local Attractions

Chicago's Local Attractions was developed as a guide for tourists and those new to Chicago and want to explore all of what Chicago has to offer! Search by attractions or by locally famous restaurants in the city!

About The Developer

CLA was developed by Mario Aranda Jr., a senior studying Mathematics and Computer Science at University of Illinois at Chicago.

This app was made as a final assessments of UIC's IT 202 course to represent the progress of learning:

  • Includes at least 3 screens
  • UI built using Material Components for the Web
  • Retrieves data from at least two different endpoints
  • Includes a visualization (map, chart, or ?)
  • Stores and retrieves data from the IndexedDB
  • Utilizes some hardware feature (sensor, camera, microphone, etc)
  • Includes a Service Worker
  • Functions offline (due to cached assets)
  • Installs on device (due to Web App Manifest)
